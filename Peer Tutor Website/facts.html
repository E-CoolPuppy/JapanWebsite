Did you know...?

Japan is nicknamed "The Land of the Rising Sun".

They hosted the 1964 Summer Olympics, and will host it again in 2020.

It has a population of 126,839,927 people as of July 17, 2019, making it the 11th most populated country in the world.

Japan is experiencing a declining birth rate. In fact, there are currently more seniors than children in Japan.

It has the most number of vending machines in the world. There are about 1 vending machine per every 23 people.
