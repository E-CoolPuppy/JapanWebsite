Basics

  • Hello = Konnichiwa (こんにちは)
  • Good morning (Formal) = Ohayō gozaimasu (おはよう ございます)
  • Thank you = Arigatō (ありがとう)
  • Sorry (Polite) = Gomennasai (ごめんなさい)
  • Excuse Me = Sumimasen (すみません)

Directions

  • Where is...? = ... wa doko desu ka? (はどこですか)
  • Where is the toilet? = Toire wa dokodesu ka? (トイレはどこですか)
